True Vision - I Reveal My Life Purpose Until You Can Feel It

  • Understanding True Vision: Leo Gura asserts that understanding the concept of vision involves a combination of logic, subconscious thinking, intuition, and emotion. He emphasizes that having a vision is not about logic but about connecting with one's highest self.
  • Leo's Personal Vision Plan: Leo shares his personal vision for his life which consists of financial independence, inspiring others through personal growth, helping people overcome challenges and making positive impact on their lives. He also includes personal aspirations such as extensive travelling, meaningful relationships, taking care of his family, having an exciting sex life, and mastering hypnosis.
  • Addressing Obstacles and Fears: Leo acknowledges his own fears and excuses, such as fear of rejection, financial insecurity, and perceived hypocrisy. He states that personal growth and overcoming ego are necessary for achieving his life goals and admits that this process will require significant persistence and determination.
  • Commitment to Personal Excellence: Leo expresses a strong aversion to settling for less or "selling out". He sees value in living according to higher values and reaching one's full potential. He asserts that he won't let the world or his lower self control his life, and pledges total commitment to his vision and goals.
  • Guidance for Others: Speaking from his journey, Leo assures that he is not special or born with unique abilities apart from becoming self-aware and developing his life purpose. He urges others to find their purpose, promising that it leads to achieving great things and finding fulfilment. Leo directs his audience to his website 'actualize.org' for further guidance on reaching their full life purpose.
  • Leo Gura's Personal Aspirations: Leo Gura emphasizes his desire to have fulfilling personal relationships, an amazing sex life, and children he can be proud of and support. His vision includes having a perfect partner who understands him, and raising kids that he can impart valuable knowledge to.
  • Leo's Vision of Influence: Leo envisions having a significant influence on the world, which forms the main part of his vision. He sees himself writing books, appearing on television, having a vast following on YouTube, and contributing more value than he is repaid. He visualizes inundation with emails from people asking for help or providing testimonials about the profound impact his material has had on their lives.
  • Emotional Connection with People: Leo pictures interacting with people who have been deeply influenced by his work, imagining heartfelt moments with them, and believes these experiences to be worth fighting for. He envisions speaking powerfully in front of large audiences, delivering his message unfiltered, and inspiring them to transform their lives.
  • Acknowledging Personal Challenges: Leo acknowledges that he plays the victim, fears rejection, negative feedback, lack of money, and not fulfilling obligations. He fears becoming a charlatan due to not practicing what he preaches. He realizes that his greatest obstacle is himself and the modifications he needs to make in his psychology to align with his vision.
  • Internal Struggle and Ego Challenges: He relates his journey to fit his current self or "ego" to his vision like attempting to fit a large ball through a small hole, realizing he needs to shed layers of his ego to move closer to his vision. He recognizes the struggle involved in letting go of each layer of ego, admitting tendencies towards laziness and reluctance.
  • Leo's Fight Against Selling Out: Leo admits selling his soul to the devil by wasting years on petty things and emphasizes his fight against this issue. He knows he needs to stop letting worldly or lesser values interfere with his full excellence. He feels urgency not to let the world or his lower self destroy his vision, and vows to continue pushing forward, no matter the criticism or personal sacrifices.
  • Unwavering Determination: Leo expresses unyielding commitment to his vision, asserting that he will do whatever it takes to achieve it, regardless of the sacrifices or hardships that may arise. He is tired of living a mediocre life that is beneath his potential.
  • Self-Reflection and Accountability: Leo acknowledges that he has been living in a manner that he is seeking to discourage others from embracing; selling out, living in fear, and valuing money and approval above his true aspirations. He recognizes his previous mistakes and pettiness, and is determined to rectify his course of action.
  • Emphasis on Immediate Change: Leo emphasizes the need for immediate behavioral changes, such as taking more responsibility, doing the necessary work, reading, making videos, speaking, and practicing healthy habits. He has acknowledged that current circumstances will not lead him to his dream impact and goal.
  • Acceptance of Adversity: Leo is prepared to endure any challenge thrown his way. He understands the process of achieving his vision will strip layers off his ego, bringing him closer to his pure essence. This strengthening will lead him to having the impact he desires.
  • Refusal to Settle for Mediocrity: Leo declares his unwillingness to settle for mediocrity any longer. He is determined to live up to his full potential and become an example of a life lived in alignment with higher values and purpose.
  • Sense of Urgency and Final Appendix: Leo used this section as a final note to his vision video where he recognizes that the intensity of his previous message might seem unrelatable to some viewers. He reassures his audience, emphasizing that he isn't inherently special or uniquely talented. The main difference between him and others is his focus on self-awareness and the determination he's put into discovering and developing his life purpose.
  • Guidance through Coaching and self-study: He encourages his audience to not dismiss him as unrelatable or unattainable and stresses that the emotional levels he has reached through his personal work can be achieved by anyone. He suggests that discovering one's life purpose can be facilitated by coaching, reading books, travel, and exploration, and offers his guidance through his videos, products, and coaching to help his viewers tap into their true potential.
  • Aim to Help Others Fulfil Their potential: Leo Gura concludes by expressing his desire to assist others in realizing their full life purpose and assures that aligning one's life with their purpose can lead to powerful and fulfilling accomplishments. He directs viewers to further resources on his website, Actualized.org.
