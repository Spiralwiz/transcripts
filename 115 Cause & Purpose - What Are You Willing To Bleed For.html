Cause & Purpose - What Are You Willing To Bleed For

  • Leo Gura's Personal Motivation towards his Purpose: Leo shares that he has been working incessantly for the past 24 hours out of sheer excitement for what he's currently involved in. Despite having minimal sleep, his drive pushes him to keep working.
  • Problem of Lack of Cause: Leo acknowledges a common issue faced by many, that they lack a cause or purpose they're willing to fight for in life. As per his belief, people end up living unfulfilling lives, stuck in routines, comfort and convenience, without contributing any value to humanity.
  • Analogy of Ancient Times: Leo compares current times to ancient eras, where people had to face hardships, wars and famines regularly. In such precarious times, people had a cause worth fighting and even dying for, be it their survival or a cause larger than themselves.
  • Role of Religion in Providing Purpose: Drawing from history, Leo suggests that religion was a powerful tool that gave people something larger to believe in and a sense of hope when societal conditions were daunting. He underscores the importance of finding and committing to a purpose in contemporary times as well.
  • Importance of Valuing Higher Objectives: Leo suggests valuing elements like beauty by creating something beautiful, truth by seeking it diligently, love and compassion by giving selflessly, justice by contributing to the betterment of society, and creativity by producing and appreciating art and culture. He encourages viewers to put their best effort into serving these intrinsic values.
  • Call to Action and Drive towards Cause & Purpose: Towards the end, Leo prompts viewers to identify what they are passionate about and be willing to work tirelessly towards it. He empathizes with the challenges associated with this quest but maintains that it's worth it for living a fulfilling life. He emphasizes the importance of developing an understanding of psychology, cultivating motivation, and striving for an extraordinary life.
  • Self-Actualization: Self-actualization refers to living for a cause larger than petty common values such as sex, entertainment, money, security, and comfort. This pursuit should be something worthwhile, substantial, and fulfilling, something worth 'bleeding for'.
  • Struggle and Commitment: Committing to a cause and the pursuit of ideals is challenging and can be emotionally draining. Growth and transformation often come with struggles, hardships, and emotional labor. Despite this, it is critical to remain dedicated and committed to the cause.
  • Purpose and Direction: Every individual should seek to find a purpose that goes beyond oneself, it should ideally serve and advance humanity. This purpose might vary per individual - some may seek truth, some might be creative while others could be visionaries or leaders. The essence however is to make one's life meaningful and directed towards a substantial purpose.
  • Work Evaluation: People should evaluate their work, questioning if it truly contributes to advancing humanity or creating something beautiful and meaningful, beyond the pursuit of money. If the answer to this evaluation is negative, change is advised.
  • Intrinsic Motivation: Developing lasting intrinsic motivation is key to commitment and successful implementation of one's cause. This motivation grows gradually, from a spark to a flame, kindling into a fire that fuels dedication towards the chosen path.
  • Fulfilling Life: Living a fulfilling life involves understanding how psychology works, knowing how to generate motivation and what life is about. It also encompasses the journey towards self-actualization and the joy and satisfaction derived from progress and accomplishment, even in the face of trials and pain.
  • Taking Action: Identifying one's cause and taking bold steps towards achieving it is important. Not only should one prioritize and commit to the cause, but also take considerable action towards this pursuit.
  • Website Promotion: To assist in the journey of self-actualization, Leo's website - Actualized.org - offers resources, tools, and knowledge. There are exclusive newsletter updates, articles, and opportunities to win free coaching hours. By signing up, it signifies commitment to self-actualization and the willingness to put in the necessary work.
  • Persistence and Hard work: While the spark of motivation may start with watching a video, persistence, hard work, and periodic rekindling are required for continued progress. The ultimate goal is to master oneself and achieve an extraordinary life.
