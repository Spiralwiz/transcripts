The four pillars of the technological revolution

Stand tall and strong, leading the way

To a future of innovation and progress

Where dreams become reality every day

First, there is the pillar of computing

With its powerful processors and endless data

It drives the machines that make our lives easier

And helps us connect with friends far away

Next, there is the pillar of the internet

A vast and ever-expanding network

It connects us to the world and all its knowledge

And enables us to share and learn in a moment

Then there is the pillar of artificial intelligence

A rapidly advancing field of study

It allows machines to think and act like humans

And has the potential to revolutionize industry

Finally, there is the pillar of biotechnology

Using science to improve and enhance

From medicine to agriculture to environmental sustainability

It has the power to change the world in a positive way

Together, these pillars form the foundation

Of the technological revolution we see today

Their potential is limitless and their future bright

As they lead us into a new tomorrow, full of possibility
