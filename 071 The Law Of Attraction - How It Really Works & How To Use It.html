The Law Of Attraction - How It Really Works & How To Use It

  • Understanding the Law of Attraction: The Law of Attraction is a principle suggesting that individuals should shift focus from negative thoughts and circumstances to the things they desire and aspire for.
  • Example of Law of Attraction in Action: If you're starting a new business, you might have fears and concerns. Most people would focus on these potential business failures, and this negative focus leads to behaviors that could actually make the business fail. This principle doesn't only apply to dramatic situations like starting a business; it extends to minor day-to-day issues.
  • The Negative Cycle: People often unconsciously focus on the aspects of their life they dislike, from traffic jams to minor behavioral tendencies in their partners. This focus on negative experiences attracts more negativity into one's life.
  • Emotional Vibration and Self-fulfilling Prophecies: Focusing on negative aspects in life creates an emotional vibration within individuals that matches the negativity of their thoughts. This mindset affects the actions we take, potentially leading to negative self-fulfilling prophecies. The negativity doesn't attract more problems through metaphysical means; it discourages action against problems due to anxiety and fear.
  • Applying the Law of Attraction: The Law of Attraction suggests that if individuals focus on positive thoughts, they can attract positive experiences just as negative thoughts attract negative experiences. For instance, focusing on creating a more positive money situation or enhancing the positive areas in one's relationship will lead to more positivity around these areas.
  • Law of Attraction involves positive worry: Leo highlights that the Law of Attraction involves replacing negative worry with positive worry. This involves visualizing positive outcomes for the future rather than entertaining worst-case scenarios. For example, instead of worrying about job loss or failure, one should envision scenarios of job promotions or making valuable connections at social events.
  • Reconditioning is key for Law of Attraction: Leo emphasizes the requirement of reconditioning one's mind to adopt positive worry. This reconditioning is critical because humans naturally tend towards the negative, thereby often failing to genuinely practice the Law of Attraction. To successfully recondition, one needs to consciously recognize where negative worry dominates and replace it with positive thoughts persistently.
  • The role of faith, confidence, and consistency: For the Law of Attraction to work, Leo insists on adopting faith, confidence, and consistency. Practicing these elements helps create and sustain positive visions, which leads to enthusiastic and confident action. Consequently, this leads to the achievement of goals and attracts desirable situations.
  • Law of Attraction impacts actions and results: Leo asserts that the Law of Attraction essentially works by shaping a person's actions according to their thoughts. This principle applies both consciously and subconsciously. The realization of one's positive vision prompts action, which then attracts the desired outcomes.
  • Persistence is key: Emphasizing the difficulty of reconditioning one's mind towards positive worry, Leo urges persistent practice for a period of one to two months. With continual effort and commitment, individuals will see a transformation in their mood, confidence, and daily actions.
  • Law of Attraction can work in every life aspect: Echoing the earlier sentiments, Leo affirms that the Law of Attraction works in every life aspect, including wealth, relationships, business, and health. By focusing on what one desires, instead of what they lack, positive outcomes can be attracted.
  • Requires deeper understanding and multiple exposures: Wrapping up, Leo acknowledges the complexity of the Law of Attraction, saying it requires more than superficial understanding and multiple exposures for successful application.
